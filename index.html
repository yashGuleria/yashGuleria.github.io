Hey there!

Hi, I am Yash. I am an Assistant Professor at the School of Management, Indian Institute of Technology Mandi. My research focuses on machine learning, generative AI, human-AI collaboration, and intelligent decision support systems, with applications in operational and safety-critical environments.

Before joining IIT Mandi, I completed my PhD and postdoctoral research at the Air Traffic Management Research Institute (ATMRI), Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore. At ATMRI, I worked at the intersection of machine learning, optimization, and air traffic control, to address the near future air traffic demands.

I also lead a team of scientists working on generative artificial intelligence applications in air traffic management. I received my Ph.D. in 2024, with a focus on developing machine learning models for air traffic conflict resolution for increased acceptance by the air traffic controllers.
